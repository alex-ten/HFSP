Home

The purpose of this website is to privately share ongoing research analyses and ideas from the active sampling study. The Analyses section contains various sections devoted to different aspects of data from the experiment. In Materials, you will be able to find the details of stimuli and procedures used in our experiment. The Writeup section contains a more coherent story of what we think the data is telling us.

Glossary

Throughout the website, the following terms are used consistently:

  • Condition: level of informedness, either informed or uninformed (i+, i-)
  • Group: exploration sttrategy, either free or strategic (F, S)
  • Task: one of the four classification difficulty levels:
    • 1D: a single variable dimension that predicts class
    • I1D: two variable dimensions, but only one predicts class
    • 2D: two variable dimensions, a combination of which predicts class
    • R: two variable dimensions, none of which predict class
  • PC: percent correct
  • LRN: learnability (refering to self-reported learnability ratings)
  • SC: self-challenge
